Prototype (Step 1). Parsing and diff run entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server, so configs stay on your machine. Analysis is produced by an encoded rule base derived from Cisco design guides, protocol standards (e.g. RFC 7348), bug lists and release notes — not by guesswork. Version-specific causes are marked VERIFY REQUIRED and must be confirmed by the engineer on the Cisco Bug Search Tool / target-release notes. No change is ever applied automatically — the engineer reviews each finding and signs off.

Step 2 replaces the built-in parsers with Cisco pyATS/Genie's 2700+ parsers behind a small internal service, keeping this same interface and evidence model.
